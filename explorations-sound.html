Sound & Cinema

An investigation into the auditory dimension of film and how it shapes perception. Sound is often felt before it is heard.

Infrasound in Horror +

Sub-bass frequencies (below 20Hz) cause physical anxiety and unease. This section explores how films like Irréversible and Paranormal Activity use 19Hz tones to induce dread.

The Art of Silence +

Silence is not the absence of sound, but a specific sonic choice. Analyzing the "dynamic range" of silence in the works of Tarkovsky and Bergman.

Diegetic vs. Non-Diegetic Blur +

When the score becomes part of the world. Examining the sound design of Blade Runner 2049 where engine roar and synth become indistinguishable.
