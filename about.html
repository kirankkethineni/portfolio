My Journey ▼

I started in Electronics and Communication Engineering, transitioning into Computer Science to find the intersection of hardware constraints and algorithmic intelligence.

From building production APIs at Harvard Pilgrim Health Care to engineering serverless edge-inference frameworks for my Ph.D., I am driven by the challenge of efficiency. I don't just build models; I build systems that run reliably in the real world.

Philosophy of Work +

I believe in "Deep Work" (Cal Newport) and "Slow Software".

  • Clarity over Speed: Technical debt is often clarity debt.
  • Tools Shape Thought: We shape our tools and thereafter our tools shape us.
  • Minimalism: Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away.
